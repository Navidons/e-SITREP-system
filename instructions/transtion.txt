**✅ Simple & Clear Explanation: Old System vs New SITREP Automation System**  
*(Written for non-technical people – like explaining to Ministry Directors, Commissioners, or stakeholders)*

---

### **The Old System (Current Manual Way)**

Imagine **60+ border posts** (Entebbe Airport, Busia, Elegu, Malaba, Mpondwe, etc.) scattered across Uganda. Every single day:

1. Officers at each border post count people entering and leaving Uganda.
2. They write down numbers by country (e.g., Kenya, South Sudan, Uganda nationals), separate males and females, and note special cases like asylum seekers, deportations, or problems.
3. They prepare a report (sometimes on paper, sometimes in Word/Excel) and send it to Headquarters in Kampala — usually by email or physical delivery.
4. At Headquarters, a team of officers receives **all 60+ reports**, then spends many hours **manually copying and combining** everything into **one big Situation Report (SITREP)**.
5. They do the same for the **Weekly Report** — even more work, with Excel sheets updated by hand.

**Problems with the Old System:**
- Very slow and tiring.
- Easy to make mistakes (wrong totals, missing stations).
- Takes too much staff time.
- Hard to find old reports quickly.
- No clear tracking of who entered what data.
- Delays in decision-making for top management.

It works, but it is outdated and stressful — like using a bicycle for long-distance delivery in 2026.

---

### **The New System – Border SITREP Automation System**  
**(A Complete 360-Degree View)**

Think of the new system as a **smart, secure digital office** that connects all border posts to Headquarters like a well-organized team working together in real time.

#### **1. What the System Looks Like (The Web Portal)**
- It is a **professional website** (accessible on computers and phones) that authorized officers can log into with their username and password.
- Clean, simple screens — no complicated buttons.
- Works even with slow internet and has offline mode (you can enter data without internet and it syncs later).

#### **2. Who Uses the System & What They Can Do**

| User Type              | Where They Work     | What They Can Do (Simple Terms) |
|------------------------|---------------------|---------------------------------|
| **Station Inputter**   | Border Posts        | Enter daily arrivals, departures, and incidents for **their station only** |
| **Cluster Supervisor** | Regional Clusters   | Check reports from several nearby stations |
| **HQ Reviewer / Verifier** | Headquarters   | Check, correct, and verify reports from all stations |
| **HQ Authoriser**      | Headquarters        | Give final approval and generate the official report |
| **Admin**              | Headquarters        | Manage users, assign rights, and export all reports |

**Important Security Rule**: No one can see or change what they are not supposed to. A border officer at Busia cannot see Entebbe’s data, and only HQ Authorisers can release the final national report.

#### **3. How the New System Works (Step by Step – Daily Flow)**

1. **Morning / Day at the Border**  
   An officer opens the system, selects their station, and enters data (arrivals, departures, asylum seekers, problems, etc.) using simple forms. The system automatically adds up totals.

2. **End of Day (by 2:00 PM)**  
   The officer submits the report. The system sends a notification to HQ.

3. **At Headquarters**  
   - Reviewers check the report.  
   - Verifiers confirm the numbers are correct.  
   - Authoriser gives final approval.

4. **One-Click Magic**  
   The Authoriser clicks **“Generate Daily SITREP”** → The system automatically creates the **official consolidated report** (exactly like the current PDF you showed me) showing **all stations** neatly organized.

5. **Weekly Reports**  
   Every week, the system automatically combines 7 days of data and produces the full Excel weekly summary with grand totals — no more manual copying.

#### **4. What the System Produces (Outputs)**
- Individual Station Daily Report (PDF)
- National Consolidated Daily SITREP (PDF – exact current format)
- Weekly Summary Report (Excel + PDF)
- Historical records that can be searched easily
- Dashboards showing trends (e.g., which border is busiest)

#### **5. Security & Professional Features**
- Every action is recorded (who entered what, who approved, when).
- Data is protected like bank information.
- Only authorized people can access it.
- Works reliably even if internet goes off at remote borders.

---

### **Key Benefits of the New System (Why It Is Much Better)**

- **Saves Time**: What used to take many hours at HQ now takes minutes.
- **Reduces Errors**: Automatic calculations and validations.
- **Faster Decisions**: Top management gets accurate reports on the same day.
- **Transparency**: Clear record of everything (good for accountability).
- **Scalable**: Easy to add new border posts in future.
- **Professional**: Shows that Uganda is modernizing its immigration systems.
- **Future-Ready**: Can later connect with PISCES, MIDAS, and other government systems.
